How To Play
The world of entrepreneurship is large, full of possibility, and--most of all-- random. The best method to teach you of the process behind and entrepreneurial venture is, and always will be, experience.

In this simulation, you take the role of an entrepreneur who is in the process of starting their own business. In order to begin your process, though, you must have the courage to pursue and market a new idea or invention to the public.

You will also need money. Fortunately, you start with enough to begin the pursuit of one venture, but you can stay at your day job for longer in order to acquire more starting funds.

As you go through your venture, you will fail. A lot. But after failure comes success.
