Basic Rules

The main objective of this game is to obtain more money than all the other players. Each player will start with an equal amount of money to help them begin. The idea is to create products and take them thru the process an entrepreneur would go through. There are nine different phases a product will go through idea, design, prototype, testing prototype, development, testing prerelease product, deploy, testing postrelease product, and maintenance. During a round a player will have a choice to do nothing, create a product, add employees, or move to the next product phase. Once you end your turn you will either gain money or lose money. Next the other player will have to do the same thing during their turn and so on until the final round is over
