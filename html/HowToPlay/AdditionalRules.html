Additional Rules
At the beginning of the game you can choice to do nothing each round and you will gain a set amount of money each round. This can be very benefitial at first since you might not have enough money to create your first product.

Once your product is created you can hire different people to help you at each phase. There is a cost for each person you hire, so might want to be careful not to hire to many people. The people you hire will help you during certain phases of your product.

Throughout the game there will be random events that can either help you or make things more difficult. For example you can get a tax break and increase your fund. Or the power goes out in your building and you need to send workers home.
